Rolls-Royce was a British luxury car and later an manufacturing business established in 1904 in Manchester, United Kingdom by Charles Rolls and Henry Royce. It has built an enduring reputation for manufacture of engines for defence and civil aircraft.

“Strive for perfection in everything you do. Take the best that exists and make it better. When it does not exist, design it.” This sentence by Henry Royce, who founded the company together with Charles Rolls, is the brand's motto, which is valid until today.
