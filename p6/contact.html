Frequently Asked Questions

Automobili Rolls-Royce is pleased to offer attentive service that meets all your needs. For further information, please contact us over the phone or use our dedicated online form. Here below you can find some FAQs. If you can not find what you are looking for, you can contact us by filling the contact form.

Do you offer factory tours of Goodwood?

Unfortunately, we no longer offer a public tour programme. Should this change in the future,we will update our website.

Can I have a brochure?

All brochures can be viewed and downloaded within the model pages online or within enquiries. We no onger produce printed brochures.

Any jobs available in Rolls-Royce

All currently available jobs are published in the careers, where you can apply for a position directly.

Can I collaborate with Rolls-Royce Motor Cars?

Unfortunately, due the volume of request we receive of this nature we cannot accept collaboration requests.

Looking for nearest authorised Rolls-Royce dealer

To locate your nearest Rolls-Royce Motor Cars authorized dealership by selecting 'Enquiry' then 'Find a Dealer' from the top right hand side.

Where can I get Rolls-Royce gadgets
